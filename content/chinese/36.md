**（一）**  
主 我愿更深认识祢 主 我愿遵行祢旨意  
负祢的轭 学祢的样式 背起十架跟随祢  
  
**（二）**  
主 我愿一生敬拜祢 主 我愿尊荣祢圣名  
如同香膏为祢倾倒 满足祢心意  
  
**（副）**  
让世人得见祢荣美 让世人得听祢声音  
让世人得尝祢救恩 我献上自己  
  
**（三）**  
主 我愿更加亲近祢 主 我愿贴近祢的心  
愿祢荣耀在我身彰显 愿祢国度降临  
  
**（副）**  
让世人得见祢荣美 让世人得听祢声音  
让世人得尝祢救恩 我献上自己，我献上自己！  

*~ English ~*  
**Verse 1**  
Lord, it’s You that I want to know,  
Going where You would have me go,  
Learning Your ways and bearing Your yoke,  
Carrying my cross after You!  
  
**Verse 2**  
All my life I will worship You,  
Giving all glory unto You,  
Pouring myself like fragrant perfume,  
Lord, Your will I shall do!  
  
**Chorus**  
So that all may know Your story,  
Hear Your voice and taste Your grace,  
So that all may see Your glory,  
Lord, make use of me!  
  
**Verse 3**  
Draw me near, nearer unto You,  
Let my heart join itself to You.  
Lord, in my life may Your light shine through;  
May Your kingdom come soon!
